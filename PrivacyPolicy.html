Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

We collect personal information such as your name, email, job preferences, and any other information provided during the registration process.

Your information is used to improve the job search experience, match job opportunities, and provide personalized recommendations.

Your data is stored securely using encryption and access control mechanisms to ensure that your information is safe.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@jobportal.com.
